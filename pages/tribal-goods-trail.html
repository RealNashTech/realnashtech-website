Project

Tribal Goods Trail

Tribal Goods Trail is a visibility and accountability platform that supports Indigenous goods and makers while honoring cultural protocols and community governance.

Coming soon to the Apple App Store and Google Play.

Purpose

The project creates verified pathways for people to find goods and understand provenance without forcing communities to give up control. It balances visibility with consent and context.

Guiding principles

  • Community-led oversight with clear accountability for how information is shared.
  • Respect for cultural boundaries, including what should not be digitized or surfaced.
  • Traceable provenance that protects makers without turning tradition into a product.

How it avoids extraction

Tribal Goods Trail does not monetize community data and does not expose sensitive cultural information. Visibility is granted on the community's terms, with a focus on accountability rather than extraction.
