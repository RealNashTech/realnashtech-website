Project

Nash

Nash is grant discovery and pipeline infrastructure for organizations that need clarity, consistency, and a reliable path from research to submission.

Visit Nash

What it does

Nash helps teams find relevant opportunities, track requirements, and document decision trails without turning the process into a black box. It favors stable workflows so grants work stays focused, explainable, and repeatable.

How it works

  • Transparent criteria and metadata so teams can explain why a grant was pursued.
  • Structured pipelines that make handoffs and follow-up work visible and accountable.
  • Operational design that prioritizes reliable delivery over novelty.

What it is not

Nash is not a marketing funnel, a social network, or an ad-driven service. It does not sell user data or position grantmaking as a competitive game. The focus is careful stewardship of public-interest work.
