Public-interest technology studio

Technology built for people and organizations that are too often overlooked.

This work exists to make essential systems clearer and more dependable for nonprofits, community groups, and public-interest organizations. The focus is steady infrastructure, built with care and accountability rather than scale.

Why this work exists

Many organizations working closest to real needs operate with limited time and uncertain systems. We build tools that bring clarity to grant pipelines, public records, and cultural provenance so the work can be trusted and sustained.
