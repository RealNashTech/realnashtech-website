How I support organizations

My work is centered on building and maintaining practical technology for nonprofits, community groups, and public-interest organizations. Engagements are collaborative and scoped to real needs, not generic packages.

Web and platform development

Design and development of clear, durable websites and internal tools focused on accessibility, maintainability, and long-term use rather than novelty.

Mobile application development

Development of cross-platform mobile applications for community resources, directories, and service discovery, with an emphasis on accuracy, privacy, and respectful representation.

Data collection and structuring

Custom data discovery, scraping, normalization, and categorization from public or organizational sources to make information usable, searchable, and auditable.

Grant systems and workflow support

Support for grant discovery, tracking, and internal workflows, including tooling, data organization, and process design aligned with real nonprofit operations.

Partnership and technical advising

Technical collaboration with organizations, coalitions, or partners to evaluate tools, data practices, and system design decisions before implementation.
